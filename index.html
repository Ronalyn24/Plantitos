Essential Steps for Healthy
Plant Growth

01

Choose the Right Plant

Select plants that suit your climate, light conditions, and space to ensure they thrive.

02

Prepare the Soil

Use nutrient-rich, well-draining soil that matches your plant's needs for optimal growth.

03

Provide Proper Light

Place your plants in areas where they receive the correct amount of sunlight, whether it's full sun or indirect light.

04

Water Consistently

Keep the soil moist but not soggy. Adjust watering based on the plant's specific water needs and climate.

05

Feed with Fertilizer

Use the right type of fertilizer to provide essential nutrients, helping your plants grow strong and healthy.

06

Monitor for Pests

Regularly check for pests or diseases and treat promptly to avoid damage and keep your plants safe.

FAQ About Choosing
and Caring for Plants

What types of plants do you sell?

We offer a wide variety of indoor plants, including low-light plants, succulents, flowering plants, air-purifying plants, and more. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced plant parent, we have something to suit every space and skill level.

How are the plants packaged for delivery?

Our plants are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely. Each plant is secured in a protective box with moisture packs to keep it hydrated during transit. We also provide guidance on unpacking and settling your new plant into its new home.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

We strive for quality and careful handling, but if your plant arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Send us a photo, and we’ll work to make it right with a replacement or a refund.

Do you offer any guarantees on your plants?

Yes, we offer a 30-day guarantee on all plants. If your plant isn’t thriving despite following our care instructions, contact us, and we’ll help troubleshoot or arrange a replacement.

How often do I need to water my plants?

Each plant has unique watering needs. For example, succulents require less frequent watering, while tropical plants may need water weekly. Check each plant’s care guide on our site, or sign up for watering reminders via email!

Do you ship plants nationwide?

We ship plants across the country. However, for certain locations with extreme temperatures, we may adjust shipping dates to ensure your plants arrive safely.

How do I take care of my plant after it arrives?

Each plant comes with a care card that includes basic instructions. Follow these, and check our online care guides for more detailed information. You can also contact us with any questions!

What if I don’t have much sunlight in my home?

No worries! We have many low-light plants that thrive in indirect or minimal sunlight, such as snake plants, pothos, and ZZ plants. These options are perfect for spaces with limited natural light.

What if I need help after I purchase my plant?

We’re here to help! Reach out via our contact page or join our plant community on social media for ongoing support, tips, and inspiration. Our team is dedicated to helping you succeed as a plant parent.

Can I return a plant if I change my mind?

Due to the delicate nature of plants, we typically don’t accept returns unless the plant arrives damaged. If you have any concerns, reach out to us, and we’ll do our best to assist.

Do you provide plant care tips?

Absolutely! We believe in supporting you beyond the sale. Our website has a Plant Care Guide with tips on watering, lighting, and troubleshooting. We also offer a monthly newsletter with seasonal plant care advice.

Can I order plants as a gift?

Yes! Plants make fantastic, long-lasting gifts. When ordering, you can add a personal message, and we’ll include a care guide to help your recipient get started with their new plant.

Do you sell pots and other accessories?

Yes, we offer a selection of stylish pots, stands, and other plant accessories. Find everything you need to create a beautiful display that complements your space and keeps your plants happy.

Are your plants pet-friendly?

We label pet-friendly plants in each product description. If you have pets, check for this label to find plants that are safe for animals, like certain ferns and spider plants.
